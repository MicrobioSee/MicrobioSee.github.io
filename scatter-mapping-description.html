8.2 draw

  • When you enter a string in the goups button, the scattered points will be grouped and colored according to the third column of information

  • You can add this button(“whether add text label”) to determine whether to mark information above the scatter points. And according to this button(“what to choose as a label for the scattered points”), you can choose to label the information on the x-axis or y-axis.
